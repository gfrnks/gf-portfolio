Gary Franks

Gary Franks is a graphic designer, photographer, and full stack web developer. His work has helped promote some of Ottawa's most popular venues, artisans, and performers. Package design, food photography, web promotions, and menu layouts shots are some of his contributions to the vibrant community around him.

Adept with both digital and analog mediums, Gary's approach to each project is informed as much by current trends as it is each medium's history. Through the intensive program at Le Wagon in Montreal, Gary learned the most modern ways to approach front end design and web app development.

↠

Photography

As a photographer, Gary Franks has studied in both darkroom print development and in digital editing. He is proficient in 35mm, medium format, digital, and instant photography. His passion is in portraiture, but his work ranges from fine arts to commercial product shots to event photography.

His photographs have been exhibited in Ottawa and in Montreal, published in the print magazine OPAL, and he was awarded "Best Analogue" by the jurors at SPAO's A+ Exhibtion in 2019. Please references the galleries on the next page to view a small selection of his work.
